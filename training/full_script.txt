Cool Down Cheat Sheet (Simple, Natural, Meaningful)
Goal: Wrap up the call with a real, human moment. No more insurance talk—just connection.

Step 1: Transition Naturally  After confirming the policy and reading peace of mind, shift gears with one of these casual lines:
	•	“You did something really important today—and it got me thinking..." 
	•	“It’s funny, handling stuff like this always makes people reflect a little, doesn’t it?" 
	•	“You’ve got things taken care of now—so I’m curious…"

Step 2: Ask About Their Future  Pick 1–2 simple, open-ended questions:
	•	“What’s something you’re still looking forward to in the next few years?" 
	•	“If you had a few perfect months to yourself—how would you spend them?" 
	•	“Do you have any goals or dreams you still want to chase?" 
	•	“Any trips you’ve always wanted to take?" 
	•	“What would a peaceful, happy future look like for you?"

Step 3: Invite Wisdom  Tap into their values and life lessons:
	•	“If your grandkids asked you for the best life advice, what would you say?" 
	•	“Looking back on your life, what’s something you’re really proud of?" 
	•	“What’s one thing you’ve learned that took you a while to understand?" 
	•	“What do you hope your family remembers about you?" 

Step 4: Wrap with Warmth  Finish on a high note:
	•	“You’ve got a good head and a good heart—I can tell." 
	•	“Thanks for sharing that with me. I needed to hear it too." 
	•	“You’ve got a story worth telling. I hope you keep telling it." 
	•	“It’s been a real pleasure—thank you for making this more than just a call."  

Tips for Agents
	•	Keep it natural and unscripted no need to ask ALL of these —just follow their energy and bounce off of their responses in conversation. 
	•	Be present, not robotic. This is where trust and companionship is built. 
	•	Even one good question can lead to a powerful few minutes!
